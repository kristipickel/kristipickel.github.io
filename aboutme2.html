About Kristi

Hey, I am Kristi, thank you for visitng my page. I am an elder millennial who has decided to go back to school to change careers. My experience in life has taken me from customer service in the tourism industry, through the insurance sector, website development, and most recently the global supply chain as an Import Project Manager. I also enjoy being creative, and my side hustle for the past ten years has been a lifestyle portrait photographer. I enjoy family, maternity and engagements sessions.

I have always been interested in coding and would like to do something every day that brings more fullfillment to me, and that makes a difference to others. I am currently enrolled at MIT xPro's Women's Cohort Bootcamp learning the MERN stack. See my portfolio page to see some of the projects I have completed so far.
